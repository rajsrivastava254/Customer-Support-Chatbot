Question: What are your shipping options?
Answer: We offer Standard Shipping (5-7 business days), Express Shipping (2-3 business days), and Overnight Shipping (1 business day).

Question: How can I track my order?
Answer: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on the carrier's website to track your package.

Question: What is your return policy?
Answer: You can return any item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Please visit our returns page to start the process.

Question: How do I reset my password?
Answer: To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

Question: Do you ship internationally?
Answer: Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please proceed to checkout to see the options available for your region.

Question: What payment methods do you accept?
Answer: We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also accept payments through PayPal and Apple Pay.

Question: How long does it take to process a refund?
Answer: Once we receive your returned item, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process the refund. The funds will be credited back to your original payment method.

Question: Is there a warranty on your products?
Answer: Yes, all of our products come with a one-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects. This does not cover accidental damage.

Question: How do I contact technical support?
Answer: For technical issues, you can contact our support team by emailing support@example.com or by using the live chat feature on our website during business hours.

Question: Can I change my email address on my account?
Answer: Yes, you can update your email address in your account settings. Simply log in, go to the "Profile" section, and you will find the option to change your email.
